Reservation Policy

Reservations are held for 15 minutes past the booked time. For parties of 8 or more, please call us directly at (555) 123-4567.

Cancellations must be made at least 2 hours prior to your reservation time to avoid a $25 per person fee.

Special Dining Options

We offer vegetarian, vegan, and gluten-free options. Please inform us of any dietary restrictions when making your reservation.

Private dining rooms are available for special events (up to 30 guests).
